## Overview

I developed the 1likte Landing Page from scratch to present the platform’s features and attract new users. The focus was on creating a responsive, visually appealing, and high-performance site that aligns perfectly with the Figma designs while ensuring a strong SEO foundation.

## Key Features

- **Responsive Design**: Converted Figma designs into pixel-perfect, mobile-friendly interfaces.
- **SEO Optimization**: Implemented metadata, semantic HTML, and structured content to improve search engine visibility.
- **Interactive Sections**: Added smooth animations, feature highlights, and call-to-action components.
- **Localization Support**: Prepared the site for multiple languages and international markets.

## Technologies Used

- **Next.js & React**
- **Tailwind CSS**
- **TypeScript**
- **SEO Best Practices**

## Challenges and Learnings

Ensuring fast load times while maintaining design fidelity was a key challenge. I also focused on accessibility and cross-browser compatibility to ensure a consistent experience for all users.

## Outcome

The landing page effectively communicates the 1likte platform’s value proposition, engages visitors, and serves as a strong foundation for marketing and user acquisition efforts.
